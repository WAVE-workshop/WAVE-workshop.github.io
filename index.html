About WAVE Workshop

The Women in Algorithms, Venture into Exploration (WAVE) workshop aims to lower barriers to participation in Theoretical Computer Science (TCS) research, with a particular focus on inspiring women to enter the field.

The workshop will feature introductory technical talks spanning different TCS areas, alongside personal reflections on academic and career pathways presented by outstanding female researchers. The program will also include a panel discussion on current challenges in TCS, with particular emphasis on issues faced by early-career researchers and broader questions of diversity. Both female and male panelists will participate, reflecting the importance of collective efforts across the community in addressing these challenges. A student-led panel discussion will also be included to provide opportunities for participants to share their work and receive feedback in a supportive environment.

The first edition of WAVE was held on 10 October 2025 at the University of Copenhagen, Denmark. More than 120 participants joined us to celebrate the birth of WAVE. We are grateful for the many valuable and constructive feedback we received. Thank you for your support!

WAVE 2026 will take place on 11 September 2026 at the University of Copenhagen, Denmark. Further details regarding registration, speakers, and the programme will be announced in due course. Participants of all genders are encouraged to attend, as all topics discussed are relevant to everyone in the field. We look very much forward to WAVE-ing with you in 2026.

Finally, we sincerely appreciate the unwavering support from BARC (Basic Algorithms Research Copenhagen) ❤️
